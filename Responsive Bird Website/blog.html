RARE BIRDS

Some Endangered Birds Of The World

Spiny Babbler

Turdoides nipalensis; Nepali: काँडे भ्याकुर

A species of bird in the family Leiothrichidae, found only in the middle hills of Nepal

Hummingbird

Trochilidae; Nepali: हुमिङबर्ड

Smallest of birds, found in America, can fly backwards

Himalayan Monal

Lophophorus impejanus; Nepali: डाँफे

A pheasant native to Himalayan forests and shrublands at elevations of 2,100–4,500 m

Puerto Rican amazon

Amazona vittata; Nepali: प्यूर्टो रिकन सुगा

The only extant parrot endemic to the archipelago of Puerto Rico, also known as iguaca
